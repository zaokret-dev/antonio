DAILY TOUR WITH SWIMMING

The Brijuni Islands are the jewel of the Istrian coast. Book your Daily tour, and we will take you on a tour where you will be able to enjoy a beautiful panorama, but also a good drop and a fish or meat menu. Lunch and drinks are included in the price of the ticket, and a two-hour break for swimming is planned.

You can't say you've fully enjoyed the treasures of Istria if you haven't visited the Brijuni Islands. Book your seat on our Daily tour and come with us on a 4.5-hour long boat trip that includes a trip around Brijuni, excellent food and drinks and a 2-hour stop for swimming.

Included

  • Fish or meat lunch menu
  • Drinks included
  • 2-hour swimming break
  • 4.5 hours total duration
  • 50% discount for children, free under 3

SUNSET FINDS DOLPHINS

Embark on an unforgettable adventure in the open sea. We will also prepare a superb dinner for you, which you can enjoy while watching the dolphins. This wonderful experience will make your vacation in Southern Istria even more memorable.

The excursion lasts for three hours and includes a quality fish or meat dinner and drinks. The sea is calm and peaceful in the evening — you can have a traditional Istrian dinner on the boat and see the famous Brijuni National Park, dolphins, and Pula by night.

Included

  • Fish or meat dinner
  • Drinks included
  • 3 hours duration
  • Dolphin watching in open sea
  • Pula by night panorama
